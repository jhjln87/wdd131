SortGroup Project Plan

John Wendt

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

Help in organization within small or large groups, will sort by name, age, and height, for various activities. Will also help visualize the sorting.

Audience

Those who love organization. Those who love visualization. Why not do both?

Dynamic elements

Visualization of the list, adding a person to the list, sorting of the list based on different parts of the object.
